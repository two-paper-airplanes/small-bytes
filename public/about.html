ABOUT US

hey there! we're Project Small Bytes, an RI CE01 started to equip underprivileged students with elementary programming skills.

We're working with YMCA Singapore and 1010Media to reach out to our community and host the students. We're teaching basic python and data structures.

We're currently recruiting RI volunteers and reaching out to students who could benefit from our programme.
